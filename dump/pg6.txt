Page 6: Human Resources Policies (Hiring, Promotion, Termination)

Hiring:

Apex Global Investments is an equal opportunity employer and does not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, or other protected characteristics.

The hiring process is designed to identify and recruit the most qualified candidates.

All job offers are contingent upon successful completion of a background check and drug screening.

Promotion:

Promotions are based on merit, performance, and potential.

The promotion process is transparent and equitable.

Employees are encouraged to seek opportunities for professional development and advancement.

Termination:

Employment with Apex Global Investments is at-will, meaning that either the employee or the company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause.

The company may terminate an employee for cause, including but not limited to misconduct, poor performance, or violation of company policy.

Involuntary terminations are handled with sensitivity and respect.

Employees who are terminated are entitled to receive any compensation and benefits that are required by law or company policy.

Performance Reviews:

All employees will receive regular performance reviews to provide feedback on their performance and identify areas for improvement. The frequency of reviews will be determined by the employee's role and department.
