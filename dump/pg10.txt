Page 10: Crisis Management & Disaster Recovery

Crisis Management Plan:

Apex Global Investments has a comprehensive crisis management plan to address potential emergencies, such as natural disasters, cyberattacks, and financial crises. The plan outlines the roles and responsibilities of key personnel and provides procedures for communication, coordination, and response.

Disaster Recovery Plan:

The Disaster Recovery Plan outlines procedures for restoring critical business operations in the event of a disaster. The plan includes provisions for data backup and recovery, alternate work locations, and communication with clients and employees.

Business Continuity Plan:

The Business Continuity Plan ensures that essential business functions can continue to operate during a disruption. The plan includes procedures for maintaining critical services, such as trading, portfolio management, and client support.

Emergency Contacts:

Employees should familiarize themselves with the company's emergency contact information and procedures. In the event of an emergency, employees should follow the instructions of their managers and the crisis management team. Key contact is the Head of Security, James O'Connell.
