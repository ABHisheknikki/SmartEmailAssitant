Page 4: Compliance Policies (Anti-Money Laundering, Data Privacy)

Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Policy:

Apex Global Investments is committed to preventing money laundering and combating the financing of terrorism. We adhere to all applicable AML laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where we operate. Our AML program includes:

Customer Due Diligence (CDD): Verifying the identity of our clients and understanding the nature of their business.

Know Your Customer (KYC): Collecting and analyzing information about our clients to assess their risk profile.

Transaction Monitoring: Monitoring transactions for suspicious activity.

Reporting Suspicious Activity: Reporting suspicious transactions to the relevant authorities.

Employee Training: Providing ongoing training to employees on AML compliance.

Data Privacy Policy:

Apex Global Investments is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and employees. We comply with all applicable data privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act). Our Data Privacy Policy includes:

Data Collection and Use: Limiting the collection of personal data to what is necessary for legitimate business purposes.

Data Security: Implementing appropriate security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Data Retention: Retaining personal data only for as long as necessary.

Data Subject Rights: Respecting the rights of data subjects to access, correct, and delete their personal data.

Data Breach Notification: Notifying data subjects and the relevant authorities in the event of a data breach.

Compliance Officer:

The Compliance Officer is responsible for overseeing the company's compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. Employees should contact the Compliance Officer with any questions or concerns regarding compliance matters. Currently, the role is held by Anya Sharma.
