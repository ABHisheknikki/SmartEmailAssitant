Page 7: Compensation & Benefits

Compensation:

Apex Global Investments offers competitive salaries and benefits packages.

Salaries are based on experience, skills, and performance.

Employees are eligible for annual bonuses based on company and individual performance.

The company provides opportunities for salary increases and promotions based on merit.

Benefits:

Health insurance (medical, dental, and vision)

Life insurance

Disability insurance

Paid time off (vacation, sick leave, and holidays)

Retirement plan (401(k) or equivalent) with company matching contributions

Employee assistance program (EAP)

Tuition reimbursement program

Commuter benefits

Equity Participation:

Certain employees, particularly those in senior management roles, may be eligible for equity participation in the company. Details of equity participation will be outlined in individual employment agreements.
