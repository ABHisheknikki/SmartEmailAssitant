Page 8: Technology & Security Protocols

Acceptable Use Policy:

Company technology resources, including computers, networks, and internet access, are to be used primarily for business purposes.

Personal use of company technology resources should be limited and should not interfere with work responsibilities.

Employees are prohibited from accessing or distributing inappropriate or offensive content.

Security Protocols:

Employees are responsible for protecting the confidentiality of their passwords and other sensitive information.

Employees must not share their passwords with others.

Employees must log off of their computers when they are not in use.

Employees must report any suspected security breaches to the IT department immediately.

Use of personal devices (BYOD) for company business is subject to strict security protocols managed by the IT department.

Data Backup and Recovery:

The IT department maintains a comprehensive data backup and recovery system to protect company data from loss or corruption. Employees should regularly save their work and follow the IT department's instructions for data storage.

Software and Hardware Standards:

All software and hardware used by the company must be approved by the IT department. Employees are not permitted to install unauthorized software on company computers.
