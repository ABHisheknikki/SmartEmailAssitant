Page 2: Company History & Mission

Company History:

Apex Global Investments was founded in 2008 by Eleanor Vance and Robert Sterling in the wake of the global financial crisis. Seeing an opportunity to provide more stable and ethical investment strategies, they started with a small team focused on value investing and long-term growth. Initially based in London, the company expanded rapidly, opening offices in New York (2012), Hong Kong (2015), and Zurich (2018). Key milestones include:

2008: Founding of Apex Global Investments.

2012: Launch of the "Apex Value Fund," achieving significant returns in its first three years.

2015: Expansion into Asia with the opening of the Hong Kong office.

2018: Acquisition of "Alpine Capital Management," strengthening the company's asset management capabilities.

2020: Implementation of a new AI-powered risk management system.

2023: Launch of the "Global Impact Fund," focusing on sustainable and socially responsible investments.

Mission Statement:

To provide exceptional financial services, built on a foundation of integrity, expertise, and client-centricity, enabling our clients to achieve their financial goals and secure their future. We are committed to generating long-term value for our clients, employees, and shareholders while upholding the highest ethical standards. We aim to be leaders in innovative financial solutions that create a positive impact on the world.

Vision:

To be the premier global financial services firm, recognized for our unwavering commitment to client success, our innovative investment strategies, and our dedication to responsible investing.
