Page 9: Communication Guidelines & Social Media Policy

Internal Communication:

Use clear and concise language in all communications.

Be respectful and professional in your interactions with colleagues.

Use email and instant messaging appropriately.

Attend all required meetings and training sessions.

External Communication:

Represent Apex Global Investments in a positive and professional manner.

Be accurate and truthful in your communications with clients and the public.

Direct media inquiries to the Marketing and Communications department.

Social Media Policy:

Employees are permitted to use social media in their personal lives, but they must do so in a manner that does not reflect negatively on Apex Global Investments.

Employees should not disclose confidential company information on social media.

Employees should not express opinions on social media that could be construed as representing the views of Apex Global Investments.

Employees should avoid engaging in controversial or offensive discussions on social media.

Email Communication Standards:

All emails sent from company accounts must adhere to a standardized format, including a clear subject line and a professional closing signature.
